Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 3, 2026

This privacy policy describes how the TimeWarp Android app ("the app", "we", "us") handles your information. TimeWarp is built and operated by Biteforest.

The short version

TimeWarp is a fully local-first app. All of your data — photos, warp point locations, and settings — stays on your device. We do not run any servers, do not collect analytics, and do not share your data with anyone.

What the app accesses on your device

  • Camera: used only when you actively take a photo at a warp point. Photos are saved to the app's private storage on your device. They are not uploaded anywhere.
  • Location (GPS): used to tag each photo with the coordinates where it was taken, and to detect when you are near one of your saved warp points so the app can remind you to take a new photo. Location is read on your device only and is never transmitted to us or to any third party.
  • Notifications: the app schedules local notifications on your device when you are near a saved warp point. These notifications are generated on your device and are not delivered through any push-notification service.
  • Storage: photos and app data are written to the app's private storage area on your device.

Information we collect

None. The app does not include any analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. We do not have any backend servers that receive data from the app. We have no way to identify individual users or to know what you do inside the app.

Sharing

If you choose to use the in-app share feature to share a photo or timelapse, that content is handed off to whichever Android share target you select (e.g. messages, social apps, email). Once shared, the receiving app's privacy policy governs that content. We are not involved in or able to see what you share.

Children

TimeWarp is not directed at children under 13. The app does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children.

Your control over your data

Because all data is stored only on your device:

  • You can delete an individual warp point inside the app to remove it and all of its photos.
  • You can uninstall the app from your device, which permanently deletes all photos and data created by the app.

Changes to this policy

If we change this policy in the future, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. The current version will always be available at this URL.

TimeWarp

Watch the world transform, one photo at a time.

Drop a pin at any spot — your front porch, a construction site, a growing garden — and take a photo. Every time you return, TimeWarp reminds you and shows a ghost overlay of your last shot, so you line up the perfect match. Over days, weeks, and months, play it back as a beautiful timelapse and watch your world change.

Yours alone

Private by default

Use TimeWarp entirely on your own — no account, nothing uploaded, every photo stays on your device. A personal time machine for the places you love, and it never has to leave your phone.

Or open it up

A timelapse, captured by many hands

Publish a spot as a community point and let anyone nearby add their own frames — a shared, ever-growing timelapse of a place, built by everyone who passes through. Explore public points around you on the map and add to the ones you visit.

Eyes anywhere

Post a bounty. Get the shot.

Want to know what a place looks like right now — a trailhead, a surf break, a job site — but can't get there? Post a bounty and someone nearby will travel there and capture it for you. Or earn by fulfilling bounties around you.

Every fulfillment is verified at the location by GPS, and photographers line up their shot to your reference with the ghost overlay — so you get real, current conditions, framed the way you asked. Photographers are paid through Stripe for their time and travel.

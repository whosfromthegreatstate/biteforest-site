biteforest / timewarp

TimeWarp

Turn your phone into a time-travelling photographer.

Pick a place you love. TimeWarp remembers it, tells you exactly when to come back and snap, floats a ghost of your last shot on the camera so every frame lines up perfectly, and plays the days, seasons, and years back as a timelapse — time itself, flowing over the spots that matter to you.

Why it's rad

One place, all of time, in a few seconds

A single photo is a moment. A hundred photos of the same place, lined up just right, is a superpower — you get to watch a glacier retreat, a skyline rise, a maple burn red and go bare, snow bury a trailhead and melt away. TimeWarp makes that effortless: it nudges you when it's time, keeps every frame aligned, and stitches the whole story into buttery playback you'll actually want to share.

Yours alone

Private by default

Want to keep it just for you? You can. Personal warp points are yours — a private time machine for the places you love, synced across your own devices and shared with no one unless you choose to.

Across time and space

A timelapse built by many hands

Open the map and you'll find community warp points other people started. Drop by, add your frame, and help grow a shared timelapse of a place that no single person could capture alone — a street corner, a mountain, a mural — recorded by everyone who passes through it, over years.

Eyes anywhere

Commission a shot from across the world

Want to see somewhere you can't be right now — sunrise at a mountain pass, cherry blossoms two states away, the surf at your hometown break? Post a bounty and pay a real person to go capture it. Or flip it around and earn by fulfilling bounties near you. It's a new way to reach across the map — and across time — and connect with people through the places you both care about.

Every fulfillment is verified at the location by GPS, and photographers line up their shot to your reference with the ghost overlay — so you get real, current conditions, framed the way you asked. Photographers are paid through Stripe for their time and travel.
